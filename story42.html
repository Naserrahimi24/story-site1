Mulla Nasruddin's Lucky Loss

Mulla once feigned losing a small bet to a neighbor who loved victory. The neighbor threw a party in celebration, and during the revelry a loose beam in the roof was noticed and repaired in time.

Mulla later revealed he had purposely lost to ensure the neighbor stayed home that evening. Had the neighbor gone out, the leak might have caused a collapse while the family slept.

The community learned that apparent misfortune sometimes masks hidden fortune and that wisdom often wears the disguise of bad luck.

Moral: Some losses are lucky in disguise.
