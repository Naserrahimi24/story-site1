Dream Feast

One night, Mulla Nasruddin dreamed he was invited to a grand feast. The table was filled with delicious foods and drinks, and all his friends were there. But when he tried to eat, he found his hands were tied!

He woke up and laughed, realizing that sometimes in life, we dream of great things but forget to free ourselves to enjoy them.

Moral: To enjoy the fruits of life, first free yourself from the bonds of hesitation and fear.
