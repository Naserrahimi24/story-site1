The Consistent Sage

A traveler asked a sage why he wore the same cloak and kept simple meals when fame and wealth awaited him. The sage answered that consistency breeds trust.

When the river ran low and promises broke, the sage's steady presence became a lifeline. He organized shared food, set watches, and taught the value of calm discipline.

People realized that flashy generosity fades but reliable goodness endures. The sage taught by example that constancy builds the bones of a community.

Moral: Steady virtue outlasts showy goodness.

مردم دریافتند که بخشندگی نمایشی زودگذر است اما خوبی قابل اعتماد پایدار است. حکیم با عمل آموخت که ثبات پایه‌های جامعه را می‌سازد.
