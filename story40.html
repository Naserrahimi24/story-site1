The Spilled Oil

A jar of oil slipped and spilled in the market, causing its owner to sorrow over the loss. He paced angrily, counting the drop by drop that slipped away.

Mulla gathered nearby people and suggested they dip loaves into the small remaining pool and share them with neighbors. Reluctant at first, the owner watched as gratitude spread.

Neighbors came with smiles and tales, and the merchant realized that the spill had opened a door to kindness that a sealed jar never would.

From then on, he was quicker to share small things, having seen how a tiny loss can create much good.

Moral: Small losses can open the way to greater good.

نتیجه‌گیری: باخت‌های کوچک می‌توانند راه خیر بزرگ‌تر را باز کنند.
